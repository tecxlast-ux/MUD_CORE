# Truth Discipline

Truth before presentation is the first discipline of PUNNARAJ.

Raw evidence, compressed notes, reviewed beta references, and public artifacts must not be confused with one another. Each layer has a role and a confidence level.

## Rules

- Preserve provenance before editing for readability.
- Separate direct evidence from derived thought.
- Mark uncertainty instead of hiding it.
- Promote only reviewed material into beta or public layers.
- Keep raw capsules immutable once captured.

Presentation is useful only when the underlying structure remains traceable.
