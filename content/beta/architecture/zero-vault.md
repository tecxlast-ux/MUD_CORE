# Zero Vault

Zero Vault is the private raw evidence layer.

It is not published by this repository. It may contain raw capsules, sensitive source records, working evidence, or recovery details that must remain outside public surfaces.

## Publication Rule

This repository may publish reviewed derivatives of Zero Vault material only after compression and human review. It must not expose raw chats, raw transcripts, credentials, private emails, or recovery-critical details.

## Role

Zero Vault preserves origin. Beta and Atlas layers explain reviewed meaning.
