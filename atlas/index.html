PUNNARAJ Project Atlas

PUNNARAJ is a future-proof knowledge substrate for preserving truth, context, structure, and continuity across humans and AI agents.

The Project Atlas is the public presentation layer. It explains the system without publishing raw evidence, private source material, or operational recovery details.

Public Premise

  • Truth before presentation.
  • Structure before scale.
  • Provenance is sacred.
  • Canonical before alias.
  • Intergenerational first.
  • Mutual understanding between humans and AI.

The Atlas is generated from reviewed markdown and remains subordinate to validated source records.
