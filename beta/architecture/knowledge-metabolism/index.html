Knowledge Metabolism

PUNNARAJ treats knowledge as a process, not a pile of notes.

Conversations become raw capsules. Raw capsules are digested into objects. Objects become derived thoughts. Derived thoughts may become public artifacts after review.

Flow

  1. Capture raw capsule.
  2. Preserve provenance.
  3. Compress into structured object.
  4. Review into beta reference.
  5. Promote selected material into public Atlas.

Each transition should make the material clearer without erasing its origin.
